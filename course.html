Computer Graphics – Course Material

Unit-wise content and course outcomes

Course Outcomes

  • CO1: Identify the basic terminologies of Computer Graphics and interpret the mathematical foundation of the concepts.
  • CO2: Apply mathematics to develop computer programs for elementary graphic operations.
  • CO3: Illustrate windowing and clipping, and apply algorithms to fill and clip polygons.
  • CO4: Apply core concepts including 2D/3D transformations, viewing, and projection.
  • CO5: Understand color models, lighting, shading, and hidden surface elimination.
  • CO6: Create programs using curves, fractals, animation, and gaming concepts.
